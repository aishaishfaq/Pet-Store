Our Story

About Paws & Tails

A family-founded pet store rooted in love for animals and commitment to their wellbeing since 2015.

Who We Are

A Store Built by Pet Lovers, for Pet Lovers

Paws & Tails was born from a simple belief: every pet deserves the very best care, nutrition, and love. What started as a small corner shop in Lahore has grown into one of Pakistan's most trusted pet stores.

Our team includes certified veterinarians, professional groomers, and experienced pet trainers who share a single purpose: making life better for pets and their people.

We carefully source every product on our shelves, partnering only with brands that meet our strict standards for quality, safety, and animal welfare.

Our Mission

To provide every pet with access to premium nutrition, expert healthcare, and compassionate services — while empowering pet owners with the knowledge and tools they need to give their companions a long, happy life.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted pet care destination in Pakistan — a place where every animal is treated with dignity, every owner finds support, and the bond between humans and animals is celebrated every single day.

Journey

Our Story

From a small shop to a full-service pet care destination.

2015

The Beginning

Founded by siblings Zara and Usman Malik, Paws & Tails opened its first small shop in Liberty Market, Lahore.

2017

Grooming Services Launch

We introduced a professional grooming studio. The waiting list grew to three weeks within the first month.

2019

Vet Clinic Added

Partnering with Dr. Sana Raza, we opened an in-store veterinary consultation room — a first for pet stores in Lahore.

2021

Online Store & Delivery

Launched our online shop and city-wide home delivery service, reaching pet owners across Pakistan.

2024

12,000 Happy Customers

Today, Paws & Tails serves over 12,000 loyal customers with a full range of products and a passionate team of 20+ specialists.
