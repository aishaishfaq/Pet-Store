Everything You Need

Ten reasons why thousands of pet owners trust Paws & Tails.

Premium Pet Food

Nutritionist-approved, high-quality food for every species and dietary need. From grain-free kibble to raw diets, hypoallergenic formulas, and gourmet treats — all rigorously tested.

Professional Grooming Services

Our certified groomers provide baths, breed-specific trims, nail clipping, ear cleaning, and de-shedding treatments in a calm, pet-friendly environment.

Veterinary Consultation

Book in-store or virtual vet consultations with our licensed veterinarians for checkups, vaccinations, nutrition advice, and diagnosis support.

Pet Adoption Assistance

We partner with local shelters to facilitate adoptions. Our team guides you through the process — from meeting your match to settling in at home.

Fast Home Delivery

Same-day delivery across Lahore and 2–3 day nationwide shipping. Track your order in real time and never run out of your pet's essentials.

Affordable Prices

We believe great pet care shouldn't break the bank. Our pricing is transparent, competitive, and we offer weekly deals and bundle discounts.

Quality Pet Accessories

Collars, leashes, beds, cages, tanks, toys, carriers — only accessories that pass our quality and safety checks make it onto our shelves.

24/7 Customer Support

Our support team is available round the clock via chat, phone, and WhatsApp to answer questions about products, orders, and pet care.

Loyalty Rewards Program

Earn Paw Points on every purchase and redeem them for discounts, free grooming sessions, and exclusive member-only products.

Pet Training Guidance

Our certified trainers offer one-on-one and group sessions covering obedience, house training, socialization, and behavior correction.
